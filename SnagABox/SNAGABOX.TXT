 . Object of SNAG-A-BOX
 
     Get as many boxes as you can by using your rope and avoiding the
    enemies.  You have 5 lives and you lose one when an enemy hits you.
    When hit, you reappear at the top of the screen.
    
          HOW TO SCORE POINTS & 1UPS
           ù Get a box with the rope ********************** 10 points 
           ù Get an enemy with the rope ******************* 1 point
           ù Score 500, 1000, 1500, etc. points *********** 1up

                
 . SNAG-A-BOX SCREEN
     The arrows on this diagram show where the character can walk from 
    one side of the screen to the other.  If you fall into one of the two
    pits on the bottom, you will come to the top.  If you walk to the left,
    you will end up on the right, and vice versa.

 . Hints, Tips, and Rules

         You can get an enemy with the rope.  The enemy will disappear 
          and reappear at the bottom of the screen.  To hit the enemy, 
          you must touch it with the end of the rope when you are throwing 
          it.
         You can't throw your rope if t will go past the edge of the
          screen.  If you are too close to the edge, back up.
         You can't throw your rope while jumping or falling.
         You move slower when falling or jumping.
