   III. Stages
   * There are 4 stages.  You must earn a certain number of points to
     advance.

       To advance to stage 2, you must have     250 points
       To advance to stage 3, you must have     750 points
       To advance to stage 4, you must have     1500 points
       To finish the game, you must have        2750 points

        's at beginning of stage 3:    5
        's at beginning of stage 4:    2

C. Bounce Paddles

 * THIS IS A TWO-PLAYER GAME ONLY!
  I. Object of the game
         Get the ball past your opponent.  Each time this is done, a point is
      scored.  When the point total equals the Points To Get number (you may
      change this before the game), a WINNER screen comes up announcing the 
      winner.  (These screens are slow... be patient.)
         After the WINNER screen, the game will end.

   II. Controls
         Keyboard-----
          UP/DOWN ARROW KEYS () - Move first player (green)
          PAGE UP/PAGE DOWN KEYS  - Move second player (blue)

         Mouse--------
          Move up and down to move paddle

           KEYBOARD controls will not work for the player using the MOUSE
